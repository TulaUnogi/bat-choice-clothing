Our Story

When in 2020 pandemic hit just as we were graduating from the Fashion Design Academy in Dublin we thought that the world is about to end.
Back in those days we would have never thought that we're gonna be in the place we are now! Coming out of the dark pandemic times we realised just how much we care about our planet and caring for it's sustainability.
From our combined love to alt fashion and thrifting we decided to create the very first Irish online alternative thrift store!
There's no more need to leave your bat cave to score some gothic gems anymore - just get cosy and let's get thrifting!

Our Promise

We're a small business with a big dream!
We love making our customers happy while sharing the spirit of sustainability!
We promise that all of our products (even if some of them may still have their tags on!) have been handpicked by us to guarantee that they are sourced by sustainable means.
Although most of our products are in a used/ vintage condition we always make sure to deliver the highest possible quality and to provide you with a description of any visible defects or signs of wear and tear.
Happy thrifting!
